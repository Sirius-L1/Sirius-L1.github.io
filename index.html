About Me

I am a Master's student at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Shengyu Zhang. My research is centered on enhancing the capabilities of AI systems, with a primary focus on Large Language Models (LLMs), Multimodal GUI Agents, and Reasoning Enhancement.

I have hands-on experience in developing advanced agents like InfiGUI-R1 and InfiGUIAgent, focusing on bridging the gap between reactive systems and deliberative reasoners in complex, interactive environments.

Publications

InfiGUI-R1: Advancing Multimodal GUI Agents from Reactive Actors to Deliberative Reasoners

Yuhang Liu, Pengxiang Li, Congkai Xie, Xavier Hu, Xiaotian Han, Shengyu Zhang, Hongxia Yang, Fei Wu.

Under review.

Main Idea:

Proposes an Actor2Reasoner framework using a two-stage training process (Reasoning Injection via distillation & Deliberation Enhancement via RL) to evolve GUI agents from reactive actors to deliberative reasoners, enabling smaller models to achieve strong performance in planning and error recovery.

InfiGUIAgent: A Multimodal Generalist GUI Agent with Native Reasoning and Reflection

Yuhang Liu, Pengxiang Li, Zishu Wei, Congkai Xie, Xueyu Hu, Xinchen Xu, Shengyu Zhang, Xiaotian Han, Hongxia Yang, Fei Wu.

WCUA @ ICML 2025.

Main Idea:

Introduces a GUI agent trained in two stages. The first enhances fundamental GUI understanding, while the second integrates hierarchical and expectation-reflection reasoning using synthesized data, enabling the agent to perform complex tasks without relying on textual annotations.

Fine-Grained Guidance for Retrievers: Leveraging LLMs' Feedback in Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Yuhang Liu, Xueyu Hu, Shengyu Zhang, Jingyuan Chen, Fan Wu, Fei Wu.

CEFSW @ ICMR 2025.

Main Idea:

Presents the FiGRet framework to improve RAG systems by using LLMs to provide fine-grained guidance. Inspired by Guided Discovery Learning, it constructs examples focusing on relevance, comprehensiveness, and purity to better align the retriever with the LLM's preferences.

Infi-MMR: Curriculum-based Unlocking Multimodal Reasoning via Phased Reinforcement Learning in Multimodal Small Language Models

Zeyu Liu*, Yuhang Liu*, Guanghao Zhu, Congkai Xie, Zhen Li, Jianbo Yuan, Xinyao Wang, Qing Li, Shing-Chi Cheung, Shengyu Zhang, Fei Wu, Hongxia Yang. (*Equal contribution)

Under review.

Main Idea:

Unlocks multimodal reasoning in MSLMs through a three-phase curriculum: activating foundational textual reasoning, adapting it to cross-modal contexts, and finally enhancing it with caption-free data to mitigate linguistic bias and achieve SOTA performance.

InfiR: Crafting Effective Small Language Models and Multimodal Small Language Models in Reasoning

Congkai Xie, Shuo Cai, Wenjun Wang, Pengxiang Li, Zhijie Sang, Kejing Yang, Yiming Zhang, Zhen Li, Guanghao Zhu, Zeyu Liu, Yang Yu, Yuhang Liu, Su Lu, Baoyi He, Qi Zhou, Xiaotian Han, Jianbo Yuan, Shengyu Zhang, Fei Wu, Hongxia Yang.

Under review.

Main Idea:

Focuses on crafting efficient Small Language Models (SLMs) and Multimodal SLMs with competitive reasoning. It introduces a novel training pipeline to create smaller, effective models suitable for edge devices, addressing computational demands and privacy concerns.
